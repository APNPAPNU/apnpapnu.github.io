About Us

Λscendant is a clan playing Destiny 2 on PC. We primarily focus on PvP gameplay, but also spend a fair amount of time on PvE endgame content, like raids and prestige Nightfalls.

Why Λscendant?

Λscendant is a growing, mid-sized clan with a healthy active roster full of people who still enjoy this game. Our goal is to make friends and help others enjoy this game too.

Our members are strong players, but we're not gods. That means that you don't have to be a god to join (but if you are, awesome), but we are not a carry clan.

As space becomes more limited on our roster, we have decided to focus our recruitment efforts on players whose accomplishments reflect the passion for the game we're looking for. Starting in Season 3, Λscendant has implemented base statistical criteria for admission.

FAQ

How long has this clan been around?

Λscendant was founded as Reign of Iron in Destiny 2 Season 2, December 2017. In August of 2018, Reign of Iron was disbanded to focus on a higher level of gameplay in Λscendant.

Where is the clan located?

Λscendant is a primarily North American clan. We're not opposed to EU members, but you might have a harder time scheduling with other members.

Any clan rules?

  1. Use your Battletag as your Discord nickname
  2. Know where the line is and don't cross it. We joke around, we roast each other, we have a sense of humor, but don't take it too far. Malicious trolling and bullying are not okay here.
  3. Don't be toxic. Overt racism, homophobia, and other hate speech have no place in this clan. Just be chill.

How do I get in?

Just apply! Membership applications are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
